Kitty is a terminal emulator that supports ligatures and NerdFonts
https://sw.kovidgoyal.net/kitty/

Binary installation:
https://sw.kovidgoyal.net/kitty/binary/

Help:
kitty --help
kitten --help

Configuration:
Ctrl-Shift-F2

Fonts:
kitten choose-fonts
Supports ligatures and italics:  CackaydiaCove NF (Cascadia Nert Font)
